MY STUDIES

What am I doing and where am I going

I am registered at the IUT of Dijon in a Computer Science BUT. Before that, I had in mind to become a doctor so I started my student life by doing a First Common Year for Health Studies. It was not what I expected so I studied psychology hoping I could understand how the human brain works. It was really interesting but I couldn't see myself working in this field. So, passionate about artificial intelligence, I decided to do a computer science BUT in order to have the chance to work on AI technologies assisting medicine.
